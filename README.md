This project demonstrates an Excel-based salary sheet that includes:
- Basic salary calculation

  **#excel-project2**
I have analysed the yearly sales data for Vrinda Store and created an Excel dashboard. Based on the insights derived from the data, my observations and recommendations are 
as follows:

Category-wise Customer Behaviour:
    1. Men tend to order bottom wear and ethnic wear more frequently than women. However, the percentage of cancelled and returned orders in these categories is relatively 
       high, at 6% and 8% respectively.
    2. The store should continue to focus on men’s bottom wear and ethnic wear, while also introducing improved designs and better-quality products for women to           boost their sales engagement in these categories.
    3. Additionally, it is important to analyse the reasons behind the higher cancellation and return rates among men to identify potential issues related to              sizing, quality, or product expectations.
State-wise Sales Performance:
    1. Maharashtra records the highest sales across all aspects, including categories, sales channels, and months. The store can further strengthen its presence in 
       Maharashtra to maximise revenue.
    2. At the same time, efforts should be made to improve sales in other states by offering region-specific products, such as clothing that aligns with local             cultural
